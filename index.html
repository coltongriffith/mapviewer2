Exploration Maps — browser-based mapping for junior mining & exploration teams.

Investor-ready mining maps in minutes.

Exploration Maps is a browser-based tool for junior mining and mineral exploration companies. Search mineral claims, plot drill results and projects, add infrastructure, and export polished maps for investor decks, news releases, NI 43-101 technical reports, and investor updates — with no GIS experience required.

Start mapping →

From project data to polished map in one workflow

  1. Search or upload. Search claims, companies, projects, or coordinates, or upload CSV, KML, GeoJSON, and shapefiles.
  2. Style and layer. Add claim blocks, infrastructure, targets, labels, roads, rail, power, and terrain.
  3. Export and share. Create clean PNG, SVG, and PDF visuals for presentations, reports, websites, and investor updates.

What you can make

  • Mining claims maps from live registry data — Canada (BC, Ontario, Quebec, Saskatchewan, Manitoba, Newfoundland & Labrador, Yukon) and U.S. federal mining claims (BLM MLRS) for Nevada, Arizona, Utah, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming, Colorado, New Mexico, California, Oregon and Washington. U.S. coverage is federal claims only — state-managed tenure (including Alaska state claims) is not yet included.
  • Drill results maps with grade/interval callout labels
  • NI 43-101-compliant report figures with scale bar, north arrow, and legend
  • Target generation and infrastructure/access maps
